OUR HALLs

Gulnaksha is the first right-side hall after entering at ICCB premises. There are so many events that took place here. One can arrange any kind of events including Wedding, Exhibition, Corporate seminar, cultural events and so on. There is no pillar in the interior with a high celling. You can park more than 500 cars and maximum 1600 people’s event can take place at this Hall.

Pushpoguchcho is the first left-side hall (closer to Expo Zone) after entering at ICCB premises. More than 600 events took place here with full of satisfaction. One can arrange any kind of events including Wedding, Exhibition, Corporate seminar, cultural events and so on. There is no pillar in the interior with a high ceiling. You can park more than 500 cars and a maximum of 2000 people’s event can take place at this Hall.

Rajdarshan is in the centre at ICCB flourished with a front flower garden. There are so many events that took place here including wedding, corporate, cultural and exhibitions. There is no pillar in the interior with a high ceiling. You can park more than 500 cars and a maximum of 2000 people’s event can take place at this Hall.

Noboratri, the largest hall at ICCB which can accommodate more than 3000 people at a time. This is the most attracted hall for who want extra gorgeous decoration and with a large number of guests. It has the credit of maximum events at ICCB till now. One can arrange any kind of events including Wedding, Exhibition, Corporate seminar, cultural events and so on. Instead of the highest ceiling, there is not a single pillar in the interior. Architecturally sophisticated and with the maximum capacity, Noboratri stands for the best of bests. You can park more than 1000 cars and a maximum of 4000 people’s event can take place at this Hall.

Hall #05 The latest addition to International Convention City Bashundhara. Spread over 15000 square feet, the fully air-conditioned hall has parking facilities for 1000 cars, indoor outdoor kitchen, round-the-clock CCTV surveillance and other state-of-the-art facilities including strong security. Hall 5 is comparatively smaller than our other halls which is ideal for small corporate and other personal events.
